Suggested reading

I recommend the following books:

Managing Gigabytes
by Witten, Moffat, Bell (Morgan Kaufmann). An excellent book - with accompanying software called MG.
Readings in Information Retrieval
edited by Sparck Jones and Willett (Morgan Kaufmann). A good collection of articles.
Information Retrieval
edited by Fraakes and Baeza-Yates (Prentice Hall). Basic stuff along with sample code.
